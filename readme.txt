== Description ==
Responsive, highly visible contact call-to-action that sits on top of your website. Combining instant-contact buttons, vCard import, location, business hours, messaging and more. Easily add your HelloBox to your WordPress site with the official HelloBox for WordPress plugin.

= Features =

* Personalize look & feel to suit your needs
* People can easily download your contact details
* Business hours, including open/closed indicator
* Contact form
* Location
* Phone & mobile tap-to-call, Skype integration
* Messenger intergration
* Responsive design (optimized for mobile, tablet & desktop)
* Call-to-action triggers (on scroll, on exit-intent, time-based)


The HelloBox for WordPress plugin is a really simple plugin that allows you to copy and paste your HelloBox code snippet from [getHelloBox.com](http://www.gethellobox.com/), straight to your WordPress admin interface.

**Requirements:** PHP5+, WordPress 2.9.x+

**Important Links:**

* [getHelloBox.com](http://www.gethellobox.com/)


== Installation ==
1. Upload the `hellobox` folder and all its contents to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Paste your hellobox snippet from [getHelloBox.com](http://www.gethellobox.com/) into the text area and click save!


= Where do I get the HelloBox code for this plugin? =

You can get your own HelloBox account from [getHelloBox.com](http://www.gethellobox.com/) for free.
Once you have your account, create a HelloBox and simply paste your code into the plugin's text area.
